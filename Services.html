Welcome to Moove Pharmacy

We are dedicated to providing high-quality pharmaceutical services and products to the community and Uganda at large. Our experienced pharmacists have been prepared for you to get the best healthcare outcomes. We are here to fill the gap between a patient and a healthcare provider.

Our Services

Prescription Refills

Through our pharmacy staff team, we fill prescriptions appropiately while providing with you counselling regarding medication use, storage and any other form of information that you may need regarding your health.

Free Patient Couselling Services

Our team is committed in helping our clients understand various medications, services and health products at no cost. Patients are counselled on various lifestyle modifications to help overcome non-communicable diseases associated with diet and weight gain such as diabetes and hypertension

Free Screening Services

We offer free screening services like; Pressure Check-ups, Weight Measurements among others while providing you with the best professional counselling services at the pharmacy.

Beauty & Cosmetics

Moove Pharmacy has extended services delivery to involve beauty and Cosmetics services. You can now get your best Lotions, Perfumes, baby and adult wipes among others from Moove Pharmacy.

Self Test Kits

For purposes of convenience and privacy, we offer self test kits sold at favorable prices for use by yourself at home following our guidance on proper use and necessary counseling.

Free Consultation Services

Our Pharmacy team is at all times ready to provide you with information at no cost. Feel free to reach out to us at anytime through our address.

Weight Management Services

Following the technical advice from our Pharmacy team and partners, we offer weight management services through counselling, Prescription refills, over the counter weight loss medications, guidance on exercises and lifestyle modifications.

Medication Therapy Monitoring (MTM)

Through our experienced team of Pharmacy professionals, we do medication review, follow up to have improved health outcomes of our patients.

Continuous Service Delivery

Moove Pharmacy is Open for you 24/7 without closure to enhance accessibility of pharmaceutical services to our clients at any point in time. We are also Available through our social media platform(s) and our contacts for any inquiries and consultations at a time you may not physically reach out to us.

Referral Services

In order to maximise our clients health outcomes, we give recommendations and referrals wherever further management is required.
